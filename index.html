What is Avengers Doomsday?


Avengers Doomsday is an upcoming movie produced by Marvel Studioes and Disney.
The movie will follow the world of the MCU facing the newest villain in the Multiverse Saga, Dr. Doom.
The movie is set for release on May 1st, 2026.
Fun fact, the movie was originally called Kang Dynasty.
However, due to Jonathan Majors' lawsuits, the movie was retooled to be about Dr. Doom.
